Jake Wilson

Printmaker

I am a fine arts printmaker, with a BFA degree from Southern Illinois University Edwardsville and an offer of admission to the MFA in Printmaking program at Arizona State University. I use a number of printmaking techniques: woodcut, linocut, wood engraving, metal engraving, and stone lithography. My work centers around subjects such as environmentalism, land-use, sustainability in architecture, and the effects of American city planning and development.

Pieces of mine have been displayed at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, The Sheldon Arts Gallery (St.Louis, MO), Manifest Gallery (Cincinnati, OH). Contact with any questions or inquiries.
